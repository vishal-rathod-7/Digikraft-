ABOUT DIGIKRAFT

Document Wallet to Empower Citizens

DigiKraft is a flagship initiative of Ministry of Electronics & IT (MeitY) under Digital India programme. DigiKraft aims at 'Digital Empowerment' of citizen by providing access to authentic digital documents to citizen's digital document wallet. DigiKraft is a secure cloud based platform for storage, sharing and verification of documents & certificates.

Documents infomention

      Welcome! To apply for government or identity-related services, certain documents are mandatory. For example, Aadhaar Card is required as identity proof, birth proof, and address proof. PAN Card is used for tax-related purposes and also serves as identity and address proof. Similarly, Voter ID, Passport, Driving Licence, and various certificates like Caste Certificate, Income Certificate, and Birth Certificate are necessary depending on the service. These documents ensure proper verification and smooth processing of your applications.
